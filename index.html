About Svintec

Pioneering Excellence in Technology

With over two decades of innovation, we've established ourselves as a global leader in enterprise technology solutions, serving clients across 40+ countries.

Our Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 2003, Svintec Private Limited has grown from a visionary startup to a multinational technology powerhouse. We specialize in delivering comprehensive digital transformation solutions that empower businesses to thrive in the digital age.

Our expertise spans across cloud computing, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, enterprise applications, and data analytics. We've successfully delivered over 2,500 projects, ranging from complex enterprise integrations to cutting-edge AI implementations.

With offices in major technology hubs including Bangalore, Mumbai, Singapore, London, and New York, we maintain a global presence while delivering localized expertise. Our team of 5,000+ certified professionals brings deep industry knowledge and technical excellence to every engagement.
